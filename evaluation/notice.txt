NOTICE — Image range for CLIP score

sd_pipeline(..., output_type="numpy") returns images in range [0, 1].
→ MUST multiply by 255 before using.

Plain images loaded from PNG/JPG are already in range [0, 255].
→ DO NOT multiply by 255.

torchmetrics.functional.multimodal.clip_score internally divides by 255.

Therefore, before calling clip_score, always ensure images are in range [0, 255] (uint8).
